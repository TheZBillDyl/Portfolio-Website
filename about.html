Who am I?

I am from Pardeeville, Wisconsin which is roughly 40 minutes north of Madison, Wisconsin.

I love to crack jokes and have a good time with people.

I will drop everything I'm doing to help someone if they need help.

I'm pretty easy going and tend to get along with everyone.

I started programming when I was 14-15 years old. I used to sit in class in 7th grade and do Code Academy tutorials on the Chromebooks. Slowly, I became more interested in programming so I went to Barnes & Noble to purchase a C# textbook and a Unity Development textbook. By Junior year of High School, I was creating games in Unity.

Currently, I am going to college at the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater for a double major in Computer Science and Media Arts & Game Development. I have participated in tons of game jams and created many personal projects. I also run a YouTube channel on the side which focuses mainly on management games and tutorials for game development.

In the future, I want to create video games or software for people to use and enjoy. I want to be apart of something bigger than myself that helps the world.
